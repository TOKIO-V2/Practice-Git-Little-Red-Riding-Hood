LITTLE RED RIDING HOOD


Once upon a time there was a girl called Little Red Riding Hood, she really liked to visit her grandmother. One day, the Little Red Riding Hood's mother told her to go and bring food to her granny.


Little Red Riding Hood was walking through the forest when she realized that someone was looking at her. It was the big bad wolf.

The big bad wolf asked Little Red Riding Hood where she was going. She He answered that he was going with his beloved grandmother. He told her to take a shortest way and Little Red Riding Hood accepted.

Meanwhile, the wolf ran to grandma's house. He locked the poor grandmother in the closet. The wolf then He dressed in his grandmother's clothes and went to bed.

Little Red Riding Hood arrived at grandmother's house. She thought that Grandma seemed a little strange and different.

Grandma, what big eyes, nose and ears you have! -said Little Red Riding Hood –They are to see you, smell you, and hear you better my dear. girl –said the wolf. --Grandma, what a big mouth and teeth you have! -said Little Red Riding Hood "They are to eat you!" said the wolf. In that, the wolf He jumps out of bed to grab Little Red Riding Hood.

But at that moment, a hunter entered and heard the cry of the girl The wolf quickly left the house when he saw him. And Little Red Riding Hood, together with her grandmother and thanking the hunter for saving them, lived happily for the rest of their days.
